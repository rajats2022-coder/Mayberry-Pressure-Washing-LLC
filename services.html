Service questions

Pressure washing FAQs.

Quick answers for homeowners and property managers comparing exterior cleaning options.

Do you use pressure washing or soft washing?

The method depends on the surface. Concrete and flatwork can often take more pressure, while siding, roofs, painted surfaces, and delicate materials may need soft washing.

Can one estimate include multiple services?

Yes. If you need house washing, driveway cleaning, gutters, and window cleaning together, send all of it in one request so the scope can be quoted as a complete exterior cleaning job.

What should I send for a faster quote?

Send the property city, the service needed, photos of each surface, rough size or square footage if you know it, and any timing notes.

Ready to clean up the property?

Tell Mayberry what surface needs attention and where the job is located. Photos make the estimate faster.
