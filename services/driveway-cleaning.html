Cost factors

What affects driveway cleaning pricing?

Driveway cleaning should be priced by size, concrete condition, buildup, slope, water access, and any extra sidewalks or patios.

Many driveway cleaning jobs are efficient single-visit jobs, but long drives, heavy buildup, or add-on patios and sidewalks can extend timing.

Signs it is time

  • Tire marks are visible from the street
  • Concrete has dark organic growth
  • Walkways look uneven or stained
  • A driveway hurts curb appeal even after the yard is clean

  2. Pre-treat heavy buildup where needed
  3. Surface clean the concrete
