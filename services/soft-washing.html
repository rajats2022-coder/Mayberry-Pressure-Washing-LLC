Soft Washing

Exterior cleaning matched to the surface.

Soft washing is a lower-pressure approach for exterior materials that need more care than a direct high-pressure blast. It is useful for many siding, roofline, trim, and painted-surface cleaning requests. This page gives homeowners the practical details they need before requesting a quote, including common surfaces, process, timing, proof, and service-area options.

Signs it is time

When to request this service.

  • The surface needs cleaning but should not be blasted
  • Painted trim or siding has organic staining
  • Roofline or exterior surfaces need a gentler method
  • Previous pressure washing left marks or concern

Process

What happens on the job.

  1. Identify delicate surfaces
  2. Apply a lower-pressure cleaning approach
  3. Let the solution work where needed
  4. Rinse and inspect the surface
